About Us
The creators of Fiqh To-go

Fiqh To-go is created, coded from scratch and has since been managed by a team of students from Madrasah Aljunied Al-Islamiah and Madrasah Wak Tanjong, with the hopes that this website greatly benefits students and other members of the community as it provides simplified Fiqh content.

The content of this website is purely based on the book "Fiqh" published by The Islamic Religious Council of Singapore (MUIS) in 2019.
