About Us
About us

Fiqh To-go is created, coded from scratch, and managed by a team of students from Madrasah Aljunied Al-Islamiah and Madrasah Wak Tanjong, in the hopes that it will benefit both students of fiqh and members of the public alike with it's simplified yet comprehensive content.

The content of this website is purely based on fiqh textbooks published by The Islamic Religious Council of Singapore (MUIS) in 2015.
